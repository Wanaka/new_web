Vreasy

During my time at Vreasy I worked on their web app from scratch where I created everything from wireframes & prototypes to the UI, photo editing and front-end coding, also the promotional landing page.

The app's end user are tourists who are visiting a city and wants to get information on that's going on in the city they are visiting and getting good offers on shopping, restaurants, day trips & cultural experiences. The business idea behind the app is to have hostels, hotels and other accommodations giving their customers the app in which they have all the information they need for the stay which makes for a better experience for the user.

Angustia

My Role: 3D-Modeling | Game Design | Unity 3D | Level Design

In Angustia you control the character of Christopher which is trapped in his own mind. He needs help to get out of his own nightmare and there is where you come in! Help him find his way out of this abstract, disoriented world of his! Control Christopher by interacting with his brain!

This was my final project in my Game Design course. It was done by me and four other students of Malmö University. Our game concept was to create a scary and weird game that you would need to interact with in a new innovative way.

ClayPlay

My Role: Game Design | Graphic Design | 3D-Modelling | Physical Prototyping | Unity 3D

ClayPlay is a project aimed for children to explore their creativness, both with interacting with physical and digital objects. You paint with the digital canvas through interaction with the physical one. Move the clay over the canvas and see how you paint on your computerscreen!

This was my final project in a media design/physical prototyping course, where the main objective of the project was to create an innovative connection between physical space and digital worlds!
