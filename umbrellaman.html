UmbrellaMan

My Role: Game Design | Programming | Concept Design | Graphic Design

Umbrellaman was a collaborative project with the Malmö based company Ixagon, who are experts in the field of projecting images and videos on cubes. My teams task was to create an innovative and fun game for Malmöfestivalen. The goal with our game was to 'force' people to interact with each other by communicating and work together to a common goal, which was to catch as many raindrops as possible, by interacting with their own smartphones.
