Augmented Reality Museum App Concept

Design Concept & Ideation

This project was aimed to practice the design process from user research, ideation and concept development to a mid-fi prototype that the user can interact with.

My interest lies in understanding the user's needs, why and how they interact and behave the way they do. I started out by doing research in form of reading papers about AR and museum experiences. I collected my data and started sketching out the persona of a potentiall user.
