Angustia

My Role: 3D-Modeling | Game Design | Unity 3D | Level Design

In Angustia you control the character of Christopher which is trapped in his own mind. He needs help to get out of his own nightmare and there is where you come in! Help him find his way out of this abstract, disoriented world of his! Control Christopher by interacting with his brain!
