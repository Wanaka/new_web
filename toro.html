[Intern] UX/UI Designer

I was interning at Toro for a period of four months where I was mainly working on their website to make it more user friendly, responsive and overall improve the layout. I was creating a lot of wireframes before we decided on a specific layout that would suit the website the best. I was also working on a future product (which I cannot show any wireframes off) where I and another designer created wireframes to be shown to the CEO to get an idea of how the website would look like. When I was working at Toro they released their mobile payment app in Norway and I was in charge of analyzing the outcome of the app and I presented data and information from a web analytics tool to the CEO.
