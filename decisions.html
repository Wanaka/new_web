Decisions Decisions

My Role: Directing & Filming

This was a two weeks-project in school where we were supposed to create a short-film and learn about different types of media. My role in this project was co-directing and co-filming, I worked with six other students to make this film happen.

The movie is about a young man who is uncertain of what he want in life. He needs to make a life changing decision: shall he go in his father's footstep or does he dare to do what he want for himself?
